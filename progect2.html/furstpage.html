OUR STORY WHY WE START

.

We are girls who love challenges and are not afraid to experiment. We believe that every new opportunity is a step toward personal growth, which is why we enthusiastically participate in this project. It gives us the chance to expand our knowledge, refine our skills, and work as a team. For us, this is not just participation – it is a journey of self-improvement and building valuable friendships. Through teamwork, we grow closer, inspire each other, and discover new horizons. We are ready to learn, create, and give our best!

.

WHAT WE FIND DIFFICULT

We know that one of the hardest things for us will be coming together as a true team. Each of us has our own ideas, ways of thinking, and working styles, which can lead to disagreements. Finding common ground, listening to each other, and making decisions together won’t be easy. There will be moments when we won’t see eye to eye, when we argue and feel uncertain. But if we want to succeed, we must overcome these difficulties. We’ll have to learn to compromise, respect each other’s perspectives, and focus on our common goal. A real team is not one that never argues, but one that finds a way to overcome differences and move forward together. This will be our biggest challenge – but also our most valuable lesson.
